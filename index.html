In the face of oppression, stories become our most powerful weapon. Every frame I capture is a testament to the resilience of my people— those who refuse to be silenced.

As a documentary filmmaker from Myanmar, I document the untold stories of resistance, hope, and human dignity. Working from exile, I continue to shine light on the struggles of ordinary people caught in extraordinary circumstances— from the aftermath of natural disasters to the ongoing fight for freedom and justice.
