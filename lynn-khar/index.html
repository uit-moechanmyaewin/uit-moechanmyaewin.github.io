01 The Author

Stories that breathe
life into truth

Lynn Khar (လင်းခါး) is a contemporary Myanmar poet, essayist, and novelist whose works explore the raw intersections of human resilience, social injustice, and the quiet beauty found within everyday struggles.

His literary voice emerged from the tradition of Myanmar's rich poetic heritage — weaving Burmese sensibility with unflinching observations of life under systemic oppression. Published through ငါတို့စာပေ (Our Literature), his works have garnered critical acclaim and deeply resonated with readers across Myanmar.

From the haunting prose of ရေသေအိုင် to the lyrical tenderness of စမ်းရေကြည်နု, Lynn Khar crafts narratives that are at once intimate and universal — stories that linger long after the final page.
